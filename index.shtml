Friday Night Waltz has mostly Waltzes in the dances and classes, along with Polka,Tango, Swing, and Cha Cha.
People come from all over the Bay Area, including San Jose, San Francisco, Oakland, Santa Rosa, San Rafael, and Santa Cruz.
People are friendly, and the atmosphere is warm and welcoming. Partners are not needed as we rotate rapidly in classes. Casual comfortable attire. Cash, Checks, debit/credit cards.
All ages. contact: info@FridayNightWaltz.com Facebook page: or

02.07

 

All-time favorite dance music from the movies,
    on the Friday before the 2020 Academy Awards.
        Hollywood, Disney, Bollywood, Sci-Fi and Anime.
            Outlander, The Man Who Killed Don Quixote, Frozen II,
                The Promised Neverland, Star Wars, The Wizard of Oz,
                    and 30 more, half of them FNW Movie Night premieres.

Richard Powers teaches classes from 7 to 9 pm. Richard is our dance music DJ from 9pm to midnight

7:15 pm - Doin' the Racoon from the Roaring Twenties, for 2020. You'll be hearing a lot about the 1920s as we head into our '20s.  Dance it here first!  Richard will premiere his latest reconstruction—a fun hit from the Roaring Twenties, Doin' the Racoon.  This is an ALL-LEVELS class, no previous experience required.

8 pm - A Survey of Waltzes.  This class will be encyclopedic!  What kind of waltz can you dance to what kind of music?  Rotary? Cross-Step? Redowa? Too-slow? Too fast? West Coast Swing to waltz music? Latin? Country? Swing? How to we choose? And if you don't know some of these waltz genres, Richard will teach them during this class. Previous waltz experience will help get more of these, but most of them are easy.

          

9 pm - Dance Party!  Movie music for waltz, swing, tango, cross-step waltz, West Coast Swing, polka, cha cha, redowa, salsa, Lindy, NC2S and hustle, plus some FNW classics.

Friday, February 7th.

$12 for dance and classes. $10 for dance only from 9 pm to midnight. $10 for students at all times. Cash, check, or credit card. Tickets at the door.

No partner necessary. We rotate rapidly in class.

All Ages.

Casual comfortable attire.

First United Methodist Church 625 Hamilton Ave., Palo Alto CA. x Byron, Upstairs Fellowship hall.  Lots of parking in the streets and in the Cowper-Hamilton parking garage 1 block away.

$12 for dance and classes. $10 for dance only from 9 pm to midnight. $10 for students at all times. cash, check, or credit card. tickets at door.

No partner necessary. We rotate rapidly in class.

All Ages. Light refreshments. water carafe.

We provide name tags.

Casual comfortable attire.

First United Methodist Church 625 Hamilton Ave., Palo Alto CA. x Byron.

Dance hall is UPSTAIRS! stairwell door is hard to find! on the right side of the courtyard. Follow the sign "Fellowship Hall"

ok to use the parking lot behind the church.

lots of parking in the cowper-hamilton parking garage 1 block away.
